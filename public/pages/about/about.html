MathVentures is the official mathematics community of TKM College of Engineering. We are a collective of students who believe that math is better when it's not on an exam paper.

Our Mission

Most people hate math because they were forced to memorize formulas without seeing the beauty behind them. We exist to fix that. We organize relaxed quiz nights, peer-learning sessions, and logic puzzles that actually make sense.

What We Do

  • Weekly Quizzes: Short, fun, and usually involve prizes.
  • Exam Support: Seniors teaching juniors (because we know the struggle).
  • Research & Projects: Exploring the weird side of math (Fractals, Cryptography, AI).

Where to Find Us

We usually hang out in the Old Canteen Block or the library discussion rooms.
